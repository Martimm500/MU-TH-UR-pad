
# MU / TH / UR Pad
![Logo](https://github.com/Martimm500/MU-TH-UR-pad/blob/main/Pictures/PCB%20Diagram.png)


A 16 Key Macropad dedicated to honoring the ALIEN Franchise...

and also day to day use :)


This is my first ever contact with PCB design but still I wanted to make something professional and that would push all my skills, including the coding ones forward.

I set out on making the design resemble MU / TH / UR from the ALIEN Movies and games (hence the retro look).

**For more info about the design process check out my [Development Journal](https://blueprint.hackclub.com/projects/13398)!**


## Design

First it's extremely important to notice **I was heavily inspired by Orpheuspad** (literally as soon as I saw it I said "I gotta make something like this"), 

Second my goal was not to copy Orpheuspad, it was to make a PCB from the ground up, but keeping what the thing I really liked in the Orpheuspad case, I fixed some errors I encountered in it's 3D model, and adjusted some details to my preference.

The final product turned out as a **16 Key Macropad featuring an OLED Screen, a Rotary Encoder and 2 LEDs**. The left side of the case is a Orpheuspad Fix and Improvement while making the style a little bit more **retro**, and the right side is merely and expansion upon that.

The colors of the case resemble MU / TH / UR (and honestly those old office keyboards lol)

Finnally I added a Dither Art of a facehugger strangling a person on the back of the PCB as well as some references to the movies and the famous ALIEN slogan.
